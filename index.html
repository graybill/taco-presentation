TACODELPHIA:

Here Was My Problem

  • I'm from the Southwest and love tacos
  • I love Philly, but don't love the lack of tacos
  • I want to be able to get tacos any time in Philly
  • I know others who feel the same way

The Solution I Envisioned

  • Mobile without being a standalone app
  • Curated content
  • Location-aware
  • Funny
  • The focus should be a map

Creating the Content

  • Searching Yelp and MenuPages pages for "taco"
  • Philadelphia food blogs searched for "taco"
  • Talking to people
  • Riding around the city

The Technical Side

  • Built on Rails and deployed to Heroku
  • Twitter Bootstrap framework
  • Geospatial querying (originally built this with MongoDB, replaced with Geokit/Postgres)
  • HTML5 location detection
  • Leaflet.js for the map
